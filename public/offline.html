You're Offline

Don't worry! NestTask can still work in offline mode. Your recent tasks are available, and any changes you make will sync when you're back online.

What you can do offline:

  • View your recent tasks that were loaded before going offline
  • Create new tasks (they will sync when you reconnect)
  • Update existing tasks (changes will be saved locally)
  • Mark tasks as complete
